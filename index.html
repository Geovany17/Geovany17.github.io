CovidTravellarGFS

About

Has the idea of travelling become a scary thought because of the concerns surrounding Covid-19? Well then, our application may be the answer for you! The CovidTravellerGSF helps you, the user ,select any of the 50 states that you would like to travel to. After giving you the list of states, it then then presents you with basic knowledge of the state along with the coronavirus data regarding that specific area. The Motivation to make this application came from the fear surrounding traveling which arose from the pandemic. It’s hard to stay safe while travelling and no one wants to get sick. Issues can arise whether you should be extra careful in a specific state or not because their coronavirus statistics could be extremely high or even may be closed off without your knowledge. Our application can help anyone looking to travel through the 50 united states by giving you brief data on that state along with some covid-19 statistics
